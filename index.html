Julia Levashina, Phd.

I am an Associate Professor of Management at the College of Business Administration at Kent State University.

I teach and conduct research across a range of areas in Human Resource Management. I have conducted empirical research on structured employment interviews, social media use in selection, faking and faking detection in interviews, biodata measures, and personality tests.

I have published my research in Management, Psychology, and Law journals including the Journal of Applied Psychology, Personnel Psychology, International Journal of Selection and Assessment, Journal of Business Research, Journal of Business Ethics, Journal of Managerial Psychology, Employee Responsibilities and Rights Journal, and Southern Law Journal.
